A Look into My Life


Andrea Sucre was born in Maracaibo, Venezuela were she begun to produce art since she was 5 years old. At the age of 17 she moved to the United States with her family were she began taking AP Art Classes at The Sagemont School in Weston, FL. By 2013, Andrea graduated from The Sagemont School with a 5 in her AP Art Portafolio Class. In summer of 2013, Andrea took college classes on Rhode Island School of Design were she experimented with live painting, oil painting, color theory, figure drawing and worked with charcoal, pencil and ink.. In 2014, she began her MBA degree in Fine Arts in the International University of Art and Design.

In 2015, Do to the critical economic and social crisis in her native Country, Venezuela, she had to leave the United States and move back to her home city were she began to study Graphic Design in the Rafael Urdaneta University.Not long after she began her studies in Venezuela, due to the extreme violent protests she had to flee to the "Isha Spiritual Center" in Uruguay were she had a strong soulful inspiration were she composed all the songs to her album "The Root is Love" . Shortly after her arrival, she began to produce her original music with the help of three talented music producers . By 2016 She continued studying Graphic design and developed her theatric expression, dance and song interpretation in the musical "Aire" This year, 2017, She filmed her first music video, and stilll continues on the Graphic Design Program in Rafael Urdaneta University were she is now completeing her last deisgns.
